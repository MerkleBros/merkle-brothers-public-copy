Governance
Merkle Bros is designed to be a playable card game with a governance system.
Players can vote on card attributes and introduce new cards into the game. Votes are weighted by card count.
The governance system will allow for a self-balancing game and ever-evolving card ecosystem.
The smart contract is written to track each existing card's attributes, so that players can vote to alter:
  1. Cost to play a card
  2. Attack power
  3. Health
  4. Actions performed when playing card. Each card has up to three actions (Ex: Deal damage to an enemy)
  5. Values of card actions (Ex: Deal 3 damage to an enemy)
And players can introduce new cards, sets, and totems into the game for the community to vote on.

Roadmap

Merkle Bros will be a playing card game similar to Magic: The Gathering and Hearthstone.

The game is being released in phases to fund this lofty goal.

Phase I - Collection (June 2018)

Players can begin collecting over 200 cards

Complete sets to unlock totems and generate more cards

Collectors can purchase blockchain tracked art prints

Phase I(a) - Trading (Summer 2018)

Players can trade and auction cards to complete sets

Phase II - Launch Beta (Summer/Fall 2018)

Players can build decks using the deck builder.

Players can battle in the Arena.

Phase III - Governance (Summer/Fall 2018)

Players can vote to introduce new cards into the game

Players can vote to alter the attributes of existing cards

Card creators can share profits for each card sold.

Phase IV - Other stuff (2019?)

Players can visit Decentraland card playing house.
